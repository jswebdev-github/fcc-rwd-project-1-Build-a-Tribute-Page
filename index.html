Sardar Vallabhai Patel

Iron man of India

A group photograph taken on October 31, 1949, 74th birthday of Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel(In the Middle)

Here's a timeline of Sardar Vallabhai Patel

  • 1875 - Birth of Vallabhbhai Patel at Nadia
  • 1893 - Married to Jhaverba of Gana Village at age.18
  • 1901 - Became a district leader and started practising at Godhra Court
  • 1910 - Vallabhbhai went to England to join Middle Temple to became Barrister in Aug - 1910
  • 1913 - Returned to India
  • 1914 - Started his practice a mainly as a criminal Lawyer in Ahmedabad.
  • 1915 - Became member of Gujarat Sabha which later became Gujarat Prantic Samiti Gujarat Sabha nominated him as delegate to attend Indian National Congress meeting in Mumbai
  • 1923 - On 3rd September, Master minded Nagpur Flag satyagraha and upheld the right to honour national flag.
  • 1924 - Vallabhbhai won majority for congress in A.M.C. and was elected its president.
  • 1942 - 'Quit India' Resolution passed by Bombay session of AICC. 9th August 1942 morning Sardar Patel detained and sent to Ahmadnagar jail.
  • 1947 - March 08 - Sardar Patel in favour of partitioning the country. His views endorsed by Congress Working Committee.
  • 1947 - June 03 - Declaration of partition of India and Transfer of Power on August 15 by Lord Mountbatten
  • 1947 - July 05 - Persuaded princely states to sign Standstill Agreement and Instrument of Accession within six weeks. Thus he integrated the country as one nation. India became one nation for the first time in history.
  • 1947 - August 15 - India achieved independence. Sardar sworn in as Home Minister and Deputy Prime Minister.
  • 2018 - December 15 - Last breath in mumbai. At 9:37 a.m., in Mumbai he took his last breath.
  • 2018 - December 25 - His ashes were immersed in holy confluence at Prayag.
